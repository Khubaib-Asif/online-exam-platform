# User Stories & Epics

### EPIC 1: Exam Authoring
* **US-1.1:** As a teacher, I want to build an exam from scratch or a question bank so I can reuse and organize content.
* **US-1.2:** As a teacher, I want randomization so no two students get an identical paper.
* **US-1.3:** As a teacher, I want my exam content protected from leaks before the exam.
* **US-1.4:** As an institution, I want a review step before high-stakes exams go live.

### EPIC 2: Exam Isolation & Multi-Tenancy
* **US-2.1:** As an institution with many teachers running simultaneous exams, I need exams to never cross-contaminate or be accessible to the wrong students.
* **US-2.2:** As a student, I should only ever enter the exam I'm assigned to, never another one—even by mistake.

### EPIC 3: Secure Student Entry Flow
* **US-3.1:** As a student, I want a clear, step-by-step entry process that verifies my identity and environment before launching the lockdown client.

### EPIC 4: AI & Live Proctoring
* **US-4.1:** As a teacher, I want confidence that the person taking the exam is who they claim to be, and alone.
* **US-4.2:** As an institution, I want proctoring intensity to match exam stakes (tiered pricing), not a one-size-fits-all cost.

### EPIC 5: Grading & Audit Workflow
* **US-5.1:** As a teacher, I want objective questions graded instantly and subjective ones assisted by AI but ultimately reviewed by me.
* **US-5.2:** As an admin, I want a tamper-evident audit log of all exam sessions and grading changes to resolve disputes efficiently.
